1.Microsoft Windows

Microsoft Windows has existed in various forms since 1985, and it remains the most widespread operating system used for home and office computers. It uses the latest version, including Windows 10,

2.Apple iOS

It is Apple's iOS system, one of the most well-known smartphone operating systems, and is second only to Android, as it works on Apple devices, as well as iPhones and iPad tablets, as well as iPod Touch media players.

3.Google Android operating system

Android is an operating system and is the most widespread in the world by how many devices are installed. Its development has happened largely through Google, and it is found mainly on smartphones and tablets. This, unlike iOS, may allow it to be used on devices manufactured by a variety of different manufacturers

4.Apple macOS

The macOS system from Apple, running from the well-known OS X operating system, works on Apple laptops as well as Apple desktop computers. Partial accreditation to the historical family of Unix operating systems dating back to the 1960s research at AT & T's Bell Labs, macOS shares some features with other Unix-related operating systems including Linux. While the graphic interfaces differ, many basic programming interfaces and command line features are similar.
